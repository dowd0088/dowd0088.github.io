About Me

My name is Georgia, though I go by George and I grew up in Ottawa Ontario. From my early years I enjoyed many of the art forms i'm now taking in this course, from the digital aspects to the physical ones. I've also shared a love for programing, specifically the more less complicated types... but overall I enjoy working with code in my spare time. I also really enjoy photography, as for me it's a way to freeze memories in time. Even so, an image can never convey the emotions of said memory, which is a concept that facinates me.

I'd originally tried out two alternative programs here, that being the computer science and technician ones. Though I did learn a lot, and developed a special hatred for network systems I relized that I wanted to explore a program with more artistic opportunities as opposed to technical ones. Since starting this program on the 3rd of September I've found this program and it's courses to be right up my alley. I'm excited for what's to come and hope to give everything my all for these upcoming 2 years!
